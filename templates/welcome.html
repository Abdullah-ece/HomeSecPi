HomeSecPi Project

Get your room secure with this, DIY home security system, based on the Raspberry Pi.

Fork it at GitHub

24/7 Monitoring

Give your self peace of mind with this 24/7 monitor system, and never worry again about leaving your place unprotected

Power Failure Protection

Keep your place secure even if the home electricity fail. With the embeded back-up battery the system will stay alive up to 2 hours.

Live Notifications

Stay up-to-date about what is going on to your place. In case of an intrusion, you will be notified via SMS and e-Mail.

Live Streaming

With the Live Streaming feature, you can keep an eye on your room, at any time.

Remote Access

With the remote access control panel you can keep have access to the system, even if you are away from home.

Internet Failure Protection

HomeSecPi does not need your home internet connection, it has its own 3G internet connection. Even if your home internet connection fail, the protection it wont be interrupted.
